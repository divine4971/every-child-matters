Our Mission

Every Child Matters is a foundation dedicated to supporting families and children affected by the Southern California wildfires and the growing homelessness crisis. Many are still displaced — especially children who’ve lost access to school, safety, and stability.

Right now, we’re caring for over 500 sick and homeless children, providing them with food, shelter, medical support, and a sense of hope. No child should be left behind due to circumstances they didn’t create.

Our mission is simple: “Whatever your hand finds to do, do it with all your might.” Together, we can make the world a better place — one child at a time.
